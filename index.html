Leslie Nielsen

The man who made the world laugh.

"I am serious ... and don't call me Shirley."

    Here's a time line of Leslie Nielen's life:

  • 1926 - Born in Regina, Saskatchewan – 100 miles south of the Arctic Circle.
  • 1943 - Served as an aerial gunner in the Royal Canadian Air Force.
  • 1948 - Landed his first television role in an episode of Studio One, alongside Charlton Heston
  • 1950 - Married Monica Boyer
  • 1956 - Nielsen’s first film was Paramount’s The Vagabond King with Kathryn Grayson followed by his breakthrough role in the sci-fi classic Forbidden Planet
  • 1957 - Took on the lead role opposite Debbie Reynolds in the romantic comedy Tammy and the Bachelor
  • 1958 - Married Sandy Ullman and had two daughters
  • 1972 - Stars as the captain of an overturned ocean liner in the disaster movie The Poseidon Adventure
  • 1980 - His comedy breakthrough came with a supporting role in parody film Airplane!
  • 1981 - Married Brooks Oliver
  • 1982 - Starred as Lieutenant Frank Drebin in the madcap police show parody Police Squad! and earned an Emmy nomination, but the show was axed after six episodes
  • 1988 - Reprised clueless detective Frank Drebin for the big screen in the popular cop show parody The Naked Gun: From the Files of Police Squad! And would go on to play the role two more times in sequels The Naked Gun 2 ½: The Smell of Fear (1991), which grossed more money than the original, and Naked Gun 33 1/3: The Final Insult (1994)
  • 1988 - Became the 1,884th personality to receive a star on the Hollywood Walk of Fame
  • 1995 - Received UCLA’s Jack Benny Award for his comedic roles
  • 2001 - His fourth marriage was to Barbaree Earl
  • 2003 - Starred as the President in Scary Movie 3 and Scary Movie 4 (2006)
  • 2005 - Met the Queen
  • 2010 - Died in Florida from pneumonia
